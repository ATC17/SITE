Atlantique TC 17

Welcome!

Join our dynamic association and experience the passion of tennis on the Île de Ré. Led by Loïc Vanoost, our association manages the clubs in La Flotte, Sainte-Marie-de-Ré, and Ars-en-Ré. We offer you an unparalleled tennis experience in a friendly and stimulating environment.

Our clubs in La Flotte and Sainte-Marie-de-Ré are open all year round, providing access to two quick courts and two synthetic grass courts. Additionally, our Ars-en-Ré club offers three tennis courts, a padel-tennis court, and a mini-golf course for your enjoyment.

We offer courses for all levels and ages. Our experienced instructors provide both individual and group lessons, tailored for adults and children. During school holidays, participate in our training camps to improve your game and have fun.

By joining our association, you benefit from a comprehensive range of services. Take advantage of special rates for annual members and discover our attractive offers for vacationers. To learn more, check out our seasonal services and annual services.

Join us at the Atlantique Tennis Club 17 and experience the passion of tennis in a warm atmosphere. Contact us now to learn more.

See you soon on the courts of the Atlantique Tennis Club 17!

Our Clubs

Ars-en-Ré

Welcome to the Ars-en-Ré Tennis Club, a little paradise within the Atlantic TC 17. Here, enjoy three tennis courts and a padel court in an enchanting environment that fosters passion and sports enjoyment. Discover a unique tennis experience eagerly awaiting you.

Our services include:

  • Tennis court rentals at €15 per hour;
  • Private lessons, courses for children from 4 years old provided by certified professionals;
  • A padel court, a mix of tennis, squash, and badminton at €35/hour (rackets included);
  • A 13-hole mini-golf for a playful moment at €5 per person;
  • A cozy clubhouse with a bar for you to relax;

We are open every day from 8 am to 8 pm.

La Flotte

Welcome to the La Flotte Tennis Club, a member of the Atlantic Tennis Club 17 association. With two tennis courts, our club promises an exceptional playing experience. Close to the harbor, our courts allow you to combine a lively day at the port with an evening tennis match!

Our services include:

  • Tennis court rentals at €15 per hour;
  • Private lessons, courses for children from 4 years old provided by certified professionals;
  • A cozy clubhouse with a bar for you to relax;

Close to the harbor, our courts easily let you combine a lively day at the port with an evening tennis match. Join our team of professionals, eager to welcome and guide you in your tennis practice. We are open every day from 9 am to 1 pm and from 4 pm to 7 pm.

Sainte-Marie-de-Ré

Welcome to the Sainte-Marie-de-Ré Tennis Club, a member of the Atlantic Tennis Club 17 association. Two synthetic grass courts await you for a unique tennis experience within the Atlantic TC 17. Enjoy a privileged environment close to other sports activities.

Our services include:

  • Tennis court rentals at €15 per hour;
  • Private lessons, courses for children from 4 years old provided by certified professionals;
  • A clubhouse with a bar for you to relax;

Come join our team of professionals, ready to welcome and guide you in your tennis practice. We are open every day from 10 am to 1 pm and from 4 pm to 7 pm.
